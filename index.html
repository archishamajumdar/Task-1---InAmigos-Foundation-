Who We Are

Our Mission & Vision

Founded on September 23, 2020, by Mr. Govind Shukla, InAmigos Foundation is a Section 8 registered non-profit organization dedicated to bringing positive change in society.

Based in Chhattisgarh, we operate across India with a strong network of dedicated professionals and volunteers. Our foundation holds 80G, 12A, and CSR-1 certifications, ensuring transparency and accountability in every initiative.

Our Vision

To create a more inclusive, compassionate, and empowered society where every individual has the opportunity to thrive.

Our Mission

To drive sustainable impact through education, women empowerment, animal welfare, and environmental conservation.

About InAmigos Foundation

InAmigos Foundation was founded on September 23, 2020, by Mr. Govind Shukla (Founder & CEO). It is a Section 8 registered non-profit organization, licensed by the Central Government. Based in Chhattisgarh, we work tirelessly across India with a strong network of dedicated professionals, volunteers, and corporate partners to bring positive change in society.

Our initiatives cover essential causes such as food distribution, education, women empowerment, animal welfare, environmental sustainability, and social inclusion programs.
